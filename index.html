Preserve Human Agency in the Age of AI

AI Automation is not a substitute for human agency.

Our mission is to preserve human agency in the age of AI assistants and the automation of intellectual work. We believe expert judgment is the last frontier of automation, and that it must remain under human control. We work to make expert knowledge machine‑readable and actionable so individuals and institutions can scale high‑stakes decisions without sacrificing quality, accountability, or self‑governance.
